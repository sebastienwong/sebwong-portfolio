HALLO

designed by Sebastien Wong

Babies + Video

The prompt we we're given for this project was to design for video-chat app that allowed for interactive calling between Rhys (a baby) and his grandparents (Oma & Oppa). I created HALLO, a simple video-chat app that lets Rhys and his grandparents see eachother, and gives the grandparents a few tools to help them have a more interactive and goofy experience.

Demo

Oma and Oppa have three options of interactions they can send. A peek-a-boo interaction that uses tensorflow to detect a face and add some effects to peek-a-boo. A pirate interaction that gives them a pirate hat filter and plays some pirate-y music. And a attention interaction, which uses some of Rhys' favourite sounds and some flashy effects to get his attention if he gets distracted.

This was made using WebRTC, tensorflow, and howler.js
