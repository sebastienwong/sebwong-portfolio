The Button

This button was designed to bring delight to Karanveer. Because he is very passionate about music, this was designed to stream radio stations of his favourite music genres. There is also a secret station, that has an extra surprise.

Demo

The button acts as a play/pause button, as well as a draggable dial to select the radio station.

This was made using p5.js and howler.js
