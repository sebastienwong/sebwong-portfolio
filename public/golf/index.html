Annoying Golf

designed by Sebastien Wong

Wordless

The prompts we we're given for this project was to design for mobile web-app, and to use wordless, sonic input. My interpretation of this evolved in Annoying Golf. This is a golf game where you control the ball with the volume of your voice! There are 6 stages to beat, each one a little more challenging (and annoying) than the last.

Demo

Using the volume of your voice to aim your golf ball, where the louder you are, the further to the right you can shoot. The amount of shots you've taken is tracked per stage, and is tallied in the end to give you a final score.

This was made using p5.js and matter.js
